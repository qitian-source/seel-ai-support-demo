How the readiness score is calculated

Each category starts at 100. Every open review item subtracts points by severity. The overall score is the average of the assessed categories, shown with a color band.

Controls a one-time scan can’t determine (operational records, timing, off-site behavior) are omitted, not guessed — so the score never reflects a false green or a legal verdict.

Example: Data & Privacy 92 + Terms 86 + Product 88 → assessed average ≈ 89.
